Paper Jam ./img/paperjam-thumb.gif ./img/paperjam-slide1.png Solo January 2019 1 week !format Primarily created during a 72-hour weekend game jam, **Paper Jam** is a competitive 2-4 player item-based brawler. The game was designed with economy and polish in mind, so it art direction and gameplay design were structured around things that were compelling yet easy to create, easy to source externally, and/or flexible to use. Following the initial 3-day jam, additional work went into game balance and level design to incorporate feedback from playtesters.
