About Me

Presently a sophomore computer science student at Indiana University's School of Informatics, Computing, and Engineering, I am dedicated to both the design and development of interactive software and media.

Having had a passion for game design and production for the better part of the last four years, I am currently involved in the production of several small-scale game projects as a programmer, visual artist, technical artist, graphics programmer, and designer.

Through course work and independent projects I am also very familiar with the development of software systems, as well as the construction of their underlying structures.

Graphic Design

With 8 years of experience using tools such as Photoshop and Illustrator, I have both volunteered and been contracted as a graphic designer for various orginazations, producing both digital and print media. As the Digital Design Specialist for the IU Jacobs School of Music I have also acted as a website architect and designer.

Additionally, I have been in charge of creating instructional material for both IU's Center for Applied Cyber Security Research and the Hoosier Games game development club.
