Too Many Bears

role: solo developer

Too Many Bears, a game about battling an invasion of mech-piloting cats with your ever-growing supply of explosive bears, was my entry to the 40th Ludum Dare game jam, and was made completely solo in just three days. My first ever submission to a public, multiple-day-long jam, the game was very well received, placing 45th among over a thousand 72-hour entrants with an outstanding 16th place in graphics.

Features
  • Custom dynamic-pixelated rendering plugin
  • Skill/Ability tree with difficulty scaling
Tools Used
  • Unity (gameplay, animation, effects)
  • Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator (visuals)
  • Garage Band (music)
  • Bfxr (sound effects)

Project Duration: 72 hours
