Gamedev@IU is a student-run game development organization at Indiana University Bloomington. It aims to create an active community centered around the broad field of game design/development, promoting collaboration and independent skill development through a wide variety of activities and opportunities.

I currently serve as the organization's Vice President of Programs, having drafted its original design and led its establishment as a formal student organization.

In this role I organize and run workshops and other events, coordinate operations of multiple special interest groups, coordinate content and livestreaming technology for guest speaker events, and collaborate with the rest of the leadership team to ensure everything runs smoothly and effectively.
