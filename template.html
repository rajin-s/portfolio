About Me

Currently a sophomore computer science student at Indiana University's School of Informatics, Computing, and Engineering, I have had a passion for game design and development for the better part of the past 3 years. Starting off by writing small game systems in Unity, I am currently involved in the production of several small-scale game projects as a programmer, visual artist, technical artist, graphics programmer, and designer.

At IU I have also been involved in teaching game development topics such as design practices and graphics programming.

Graphic Design

With 8 years of experience with tools like Photoshop and Illustrator, I have both volunteered and been contracted as a graphic designer for various orginazations, producing both digital and print media. As the Digital Design Specialist for the IU Jacobs School of Music I have also acted as a website architect and designer.

Hoosier Games

As the Workshop/Presentation Director of IU's game development club Hoosier Games I have been involved in the creation and presentation of instructional material related to a variety of topics from basic programming to the game art & aesthetics. As an officer of the club I've also spearheaded major reworks of its core design, developing systems to help members more effectively develop marketable skills.
